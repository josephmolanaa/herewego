## ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

For **each competitor**, analyze and document:

### 1. First Impression & Branding (30 seconds test)
- Visual design quality (1–10)
- Brand clarity — do I immediately understand what they offer?
- Color scheme, typography, imagery quality
- Mobile responsiveness

### 2. Content & Information Architecture
- How are services/packages presented?
- Is pricing visible or hidden?
- Quality & completeness of tour descriptions
- Itinerary detail level
- Photo/video quality

### 3. Trust & Social Proof
- Testimonials — quantity, quality, verified?
- Reviews (Google, TripAdvisor, etc.)
- Partner logos, certifications, media mentions
- Team/about page — does it feel human?
- Contact information visibility

### 4. Booking & Conversion Flow
- CTA placement and clarity
- Booking process — how many steps?
- WhatsApp / direct contact integration
- Payment options (if any)
- Urgency triggers (limited slots, discounts, etc.)

### 5. SEO & Discoverability
- Page titles and meta descriptions quality
- Keyword targeting (what terms do they rank for?)
- Blog/content marketing presence
- Sitemap, structured data, schema markup
- Page load speed impression

### 6. Unique Features / Differentiators
- Anything HereWeGo does NOT have that competitors do well
- Any features that seem innovative or effective
